What possibilities open up when we compare numbers
in real time?

When data is compared, analyzed, and filtered instantly, it transforms into actionable insights that drive success.

The Problem

SuperPay's clients need fast, reliable, and real-time access to their business data in order to make informed decisions, however they faced the following challenges:

Time Waste

Accessing real-time data often took longer than necessary, slowing down workflows and impacting the overall user experience.

Filters

Data filtering and customization options were limited, preventing clients from tailoring their views and reports to their specific needs.

Our Solution

We designed and developed an interactive, real-time dashboard that allows SuperPay’s clients to view and analyze their key data in seconds.

Key Features

Dynamic Filters

Terminal, payment method, transaction status, and date range.

Clear Visualizations

  • Pie charts showing distribution by status.
  • Bar & line charts tracking daily amounts.
  • Revenue flow indicators by status.

Responsive Design

Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Intuitive UI

Simple to use, even for non-technical users.

Impact

For SuperPay’s clients

  • Understand business trends instantly.
  • Identify issues quickly (e.g., high failure rates for a payment method).
  • Make smarter business decisions based on real, up-to-the-minute data.

For SuperPay

  • More satisfied, self-sufficient clients.
  • A strong foundation for future back-office modules.

Our Team

We combined design and development efforts to deliver a dashboard that transforms complex data into clear, actionable insights, maximizing the value SuperPay provides to its clients.
